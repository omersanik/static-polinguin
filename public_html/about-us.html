How does it work?

Our tools are designed to help you get started with Polish quickly and to give you a clear view of how the Polish language actually looks.

How long will it take me to learn Polish?

Everyone learns at a different pace, and it depends on your language background. If you speak a Slavic language, learning Polish might be easier and faster for you. However, if your native language is English or another non-Slavic language, it may take more time to grasp Polish. That’s why we created these tools—to help you learn Polish as efficiently as possible!
